Koala Point

Native to our flora and fauna and highly vulnerable from extinction - Meet the Koala.

The fatal bushfires in 2020 caused a tremendous decline of the population living today in the wild. It is assumed that two-thirds of the entire country population fell victim to the bushfires. Today it is expected that less than 80.000 are amongst us. Without our help and protection these beautfil creatures of our continent will probably not make it until the next century.
